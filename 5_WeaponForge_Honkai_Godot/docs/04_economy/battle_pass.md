# Battle Pass — Stub

**Status:** Deferred from initial GDD.

## Scope

Battle Pass design — tiers, rewards, free vs premium tracks, season length, price.

## Locked anchors (already in GDD)

- **Seasonal Battle Pass** (~$5–10/season).
- **Free + premium track** (standard pattern).
- **Battle Pass XP** earned from quest completion and stage clears.

## Open design questions

- **Season length**: 8 weeks (industry default) or 6 weeks (more aggressive cadence)?
- **Tier count**: 50 tiers, 100 tiers?
- **Premium-only rewards**: hero shards? Exclusive cosmetics? Premium currency?
- **Free track value**: must be enough to retain non-paying players (15-20% of total BP value).
- **Catch-up mechanics**: can players who miss a week catch up via gold purchase of tier ups?
- **Cosmetic exclusivity**: BP cosmetics never return, or return in later vault rotations?
- **Multi-season passes**: lifetime pass? Annual pass at discount?

## Recommended approach

- **8-week seasons**.
- **60 tiers**.
- **Premium price: $9.99** (industry sweet spot).
- **Free track**: ~20% of total BP value (mostly gold, stamina, occasional shard).
- **Premium track**: includes 1 guaranteed Epic hero shard pack at tier 30, 1 guaranteed Legendary hero shard pack at tier 60.
- **Cosmetics**: vault rotation every 4 seasons.

## References to consult

- Fortnite BP (canonical example, though premium product).
- Genshin Welkin Moon (subscription analog).
- AFK Journey season pass (most direct comp).
- Hero Wars season pass (same monetization tier).
