Honkai: Star Rail crossed $2 billion in lifetime mobile revenue on March 23, 2025 — just 23 months after its April 2023 launch. In 2024, the game earned $870.9 million on iOS and Google Play, outpacing Genshin Impact’s $730.1 million over the same period. This article covers verified player counts, monthly revenue trends, regional spending, and download figures, all drawn from primary sources.

## Honkai: Star Rail Statistics — Key Numbers

- Honkai: Star Rail reached $2 billion in lifetime mobile revenue in 23 months, as of March 23, 2025, according to AppMagic data cited by PocketGamer.biz.

- The game earned $870.9 million on iOS and Google Play in 2024, ranking 12th among all mobile titles globally by revenue.

- HoYoverse confirmed 30.1 million monthly active users across all platforms in February 2025, up 66% from 18.1 million in January 2024.

- Japan generated 29% of mobile IAP revenue despite accounting for only 6.3% of total downloads — the highest revenue-to-download ratio of any market.

- The game’s average revenue per daily active user (ARPDAU) is $0.77, compared to a mid-core RPG benchmark of $0.30–$0.50.

## How Many People Play Honkai: Star Rail?

HoYoverse does not release concurrent player counts. The data below combines HoYoverse’s own confirmed disclosures with platform-tracked estimates from AppMagic via Udonis, which cover primarily PC and mobile players.

The two datasets tell different stories. HoYoverse confirmed 30.1 million all-platform monthly active users in February 2025 — a 66% increase from 18.1 million in January 2024. The narrower AppMagic-tracked dataset shows daily active users peaking at 3.33 million in August 2024, then declining 26% to 2.47 million by June 2025. This pattern matches the typical arc of live-service gacha titles, where engagement tightens around major content releases and softens between patches.

## Honkai: Star Rail Revenue by Month (2024–2025)

All figures below track iOS and Google Play purchases only. PC and PlayStation revenue is not publicly disclosed. AppMagic is the underlying data source, aggregated through Udonis, Statista, and PocketGamer.biz.

March 2024 was the highest single month on record for 2024. Udonis reports $91.8 million while PocketGamer.biz cites $129.8 million — the gap reflects different AppMagic data cuts, one iOS-only and one combined. Both sources identify March 2024 as the peak, driven by the Version 2.0 Penacony expansion banners. In that same month, Genshin Impact earned $58.5 million, meaning Honkai: Star Rail generated more than double its stablemate’s mobile revenue.

The $51 million swing between November 2024’s $19.7 million low and April 2025’s $71.2 million peak — within six months — shows how completely banner-driven monetisation controls revenue variance. Months without flagship 5-star character releases compress revenue toward a floor; major banner launches push it toward the ceiling.

## Honkai: Star Rail Revenue by Country and Region

Three markets — China, Japan, and the United States — generated approximately 78% of all mobile IAP revenue in 2024. Japan’s position within that group is the most striking: 29% of revenue from just 6.3% of downloads.

At the game’s first anniversary in April 2024, Japan’s 30-day revenue share briefly rose to 33%, closing within one percentage point of China’s share. That spike reflects the established gacha spending culture in Japan, where players spend across multiple consecutive banner cycles rather than in isolated transactions. This per-user monetisation dynamic is well-documented across gaming industry revenue data from Asia-Pacific markets.

## Honkai: Star Rail Downloads and Platform Data

HoYoverse confirmed over 100 million total downloads across all platforms in February 2024, timed to the Version 2.0 release. iOS and Google Play alone tracked 50.22 million downloads through May 2024, according to AppMagic data via Gacha HQ.

Launch day on April 26, 2023 generated 20 million downloads — below Genshin Impact’s 23 million in a single launch week, but enough to confirm commercial viability within hours. The game currently runs on Windows (HoYoverse launcher and Epic Games Store), iOS, Android, and PlayStation 5. It is not available on Steam, Nintendo Switch, macOS, or Xbox.

The PS5 expansion in October 2024 is directly tied to the 66% increase in all-platform MAUs reported between January 2024 and February 2025. Cross-platform availability remains a core growth lever for gacha titles in 2025, as documented in cross-platform gaming adoption data.

## Honkai: Star Rail Statistics vs. Genshin Impact

Honkai: Star Rail outearned Genshin Impact every month from March through September 2024 — seven consecutive months. In March 2024 alone, Star Rail generated more than double Genshin’s $58.5 million in mobile revenue.

This is notable because Genshin Impact had a three-year head start and a substantially larger registered user base. Star Rail’s advantage comes from a higher-spending active cohort — its $0.77 ARPDAU vs. Genshin’s lower per-user revenue — rather than raw player volume. The two games compete internally within HoYoverse’s portfolio, and in Q1 2025, Star Rail ranked first among all gacha RPGs in Asia-Pacific revenue at $149.8 million. For broader context on how these titles fit within the wider video game market, mobile gacha remains one of the highest-margin segments in the industry.
