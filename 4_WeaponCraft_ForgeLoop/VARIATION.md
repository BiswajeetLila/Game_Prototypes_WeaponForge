# ForgeLoop — Variation Delta

A new mobile auto-battler variant that replaces menu-based crafting with a Loop-Hero-style circular forge ring.

- **Crafting is a loop, not a menu.** 8-cell hex ring. Player places station tiles. Glowing ingot laps the ring clockwise. Each station triggers a juicy modal minigame (≤7s). One lap = 25–35s.
- **Reveal is the climax.** 4-second anvil-RING sequence ends with the weapon snapping into the hero's hand. The same weapon visibly arms the hero in combat — sprite, glyph, element burst.
- **Weapons persist + upgrade.** After each wave, "Forge Again? (5g)" re-enters the loop and stacks stats additively. Depth-per-weapon over breadth.

Forked from `2_WeaponCraft_Base/Prototype/dist/BASE-A1_0.1.9.html`. Combat layer reused.
