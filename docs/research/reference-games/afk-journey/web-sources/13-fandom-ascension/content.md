### Descrip­tion

 
 Description of the item

Ascension is the process of upgrading a hero to a higher tier, such as upgrading from Elite to a Elite+. Ascension makes a hero stronger by unlocking stat buffs and new skills for that hero.

## Hero Tiers[]

In total, there are 15 hero tiers. The Rare tier, seen with Chippy and Hammie, is not available for recruitable characters. Rare heroes cannot be ascended out of Rare tier, but may occasionally be seen with a higher hero tier when used in certain battles. In such cases, a Rare hero only receives bonus stats and does not unlock any new skills.

A-Level heroes begin their ascensions from the Elite tier, and can be built up over 14 hero tiers until they reach Paragon 4:

## Skills[]

All heroes possess 3 base skills by default (Ultimate, Skill 1, Skill 2) and unlock new skills at Legendary+, Mythic+, and Supreme+.

### Special Case: Crossover Heroes[]

While a Dimensional Crossover hero's Recruitment banner is active, a crossover hero will share the same ascension costs as outlined above. However, once a crossover hero's recruitment banner has ended, their Soul Sigils will be converted into generic fragments called Dimensional Soulstones. When their banner is over, crossover heroes can only be ascended using these fragments, as it is impossible to obtain their regular Soul Sigils past that point.

Regular dimensional heroes, such as Pandora, are not affected by this conversion rule, and will continue to be ascendable using their regular Soul Sigils according to the cost table above.

The standard conversion rate is 1 Soul Sigil to 3 Dimensional Soulstones.

## Paragon Tiers[]

Paragon is a series of hero tiers that grant special PVP bonuses known as Rivalry Stats. By default, the existence of Paragon tiers is hidden until a player has ascended 25 heroes to Supreme+, at which point it will unlock and players will be able to use excess Soul Sigils to ascend their heroes higher. Paragon tiers do not unlock new skills.

Every Paragon tier requires a minimum number of heroes ascended to the previous hero tier in order to become unlocked:

Requirement
