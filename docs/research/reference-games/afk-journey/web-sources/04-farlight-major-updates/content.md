## Version 1.5.1 — September 26, 2025

### New Heroes
- Aliceth (Celestial, Marksman, S-Level) — "the Radiant Wings." Acquired through Stargaze Station and Guild Store.
- Perseus (Lightbearer, Warrior, S-Level) — "the Chosen Champion." Acquired through Rate Up Recruitment.

### Recruitment System
- New Rate Up Return feature added, following the same rules as regular Rate Up Recruitment.
- Two returning heroes featured each week, first announced September 29 and available from October 3.

### AFK Stages & Rewards Overhaul
- One-time season rewards for clearing AFK Stages (Manuals, Essence, Starlight) are now tied to the Season Handbook and unlocked over time.
- Clearing AFK Stages now rewards Select Equipment Chests (player chooses the piece), replacing random Equipment drops.
- On the first day of a new season, AFK rewards hit the maximum efficiency cap after completing AFK Stage 10 to ease day-one progression.
- "Infinite Stage" renamed to Apex Stage with fully redesigned stages and difficulty scaling.

### Combat Mechanics
- Level 2 Enhance Force added for Antandra and Lyca.

### Treasure Trials Overhaul
- Nightmare Trial and Wish List modes removed.
- One class-specific treasure trial opens each day; six class-specific trials rotate in a fixed order.
- Each daily trial grants essence for two different classes, covering all classes over a three-day cycle.

## Version 1.2.1 — September 19, 2024 (Waves of Intrigue Season Launch)

### Maintenance
Server maintenance: September 19, 07:00–09:00 UTC.

### New Hero
- Dunlingr, the Eternal Voice (Celestial) — released September 20, 00:00 UTC.

### Waves of Intrigue Season
- Launched September 19 for servers active 40+ days.
- Unlock requirements: reach Level 240 Hands of Resonance and complete Ryeham Tales.
- New storyline and season map included.

### New Game Modes
- Heroic Gauntlet: heroes temporarily upgraded to Mythic tier; rewards include Invite Letters, Velvet Vouchers, and Twilight Essence.
- Talent Trials: new mode in Season AFK Stages using Faction Talents; clearing stages increases Season AFK progress.
- Peaks of Time: added October 1; allows players who missed Song of Strife season to collect map resources. Requires completing The Last Leg quest.

### Seasonal Progression
- Season Resonance Level starts at 240; Season Equipment Levels begin at 241.
- Ten new Season Artifacts (max level 25) rolling out throughout the season.
- Season Faction Talents active in Season Legend Trial, Talent Trials, Supreme Arena, and other seasonal modes.
- Season Store added with Soul Sigils redemption.
- Season Swaps: swap two heroes released during Waves of Intrigue season — swaps hero tiers and levels of Exclusive Equipment, Hero Focus, and Enhance Force.
- Season Handbook: earn Season Milestone Points by completing Season Challenge Missions to unlock rewards.

### New Features
- Rustport Fishing: choose a fishing spot on the season map to gain Skill Points, increase Fishing Level, unlock extra bonuses.
- Magic Charms: equippable by all heroes, providing additional stats and set bonuses; primarily obtained through Dura's Trials.
- Two new Dream Realm Bosses: Crystal Beetle and Orson.
- Velvet Store for cosmetics.

### Quality-of-Life Improvements
- Auto-Route mode: now automatically grants 2 stacks of Speed Fairy buffs.
- Enhanced mini-map functionality for area access.
- Memory optimization, emoji support while traveling, cosmetics preview enhancements.
- Dura's Trials optimization.

### Combat Fixes
Heroes adjusted: Salazer, Lily May, Igor, Thoran, Granny Dahnie, and others (skill interactions, stat calculations, ability timing).

### Honor Duel Adjustments
- Epic Equipment strength increased.
- Hero-specific Artifacts boost that character's store appearance odds.
- Hero Trial Card item added to Duel Store.

## 2025 Upcoming Features (as of December 2024 Developer Notes)

- "Your Game, Your Choice" reward system: consolidates weekly rewards from most game modes; increases hero-exclusive Temporal Essences and Stellar Crystals.
- New "Grace" mechanism for Dura's Trials: replaces simple stat boosts with effects that enhance heroes' unique abilities; limits apply per battle (requires strategic pairing).
- Treasure stages reduced from 12 groups to 6 groups; two open daily in a fixed sequence over a three-day cycle.
- New core seasonal modes in beta:
  - Tournaments (solo competitive mode)
  - Expedition / Dungeon (Guild-based PVE to explore and obtain rewards)
  - WarZone / Guild-versus-Guild Battlefield mode
- Clashfronts revamp and Arcane Labyrinth updates.
- "Less Grindy Sundays" — Sunday gameplay redesigned to feel more relaxed.
