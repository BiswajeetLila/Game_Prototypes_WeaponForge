## Overview

The arrival of higher floors in Dura's Trial marks a pivotal moment for global server players — they can finally craft and refine Mythic Magic Charms, offering not just raw stat boosts but powerful set effects that can dramatically elevate performance across all game modes.

Charms have 4 quality tiers: Elite, Epic, Legendary, and Mythic. Mythic Charms can be upgraded further to V+ and V++ using currency earned through salvaging duplicate charms. Having 3 Mythic charms upgraded to at least V+ earns an additional "Type Bonus."

The guide emphasizes: focus refining resources on core Dream Realm carries first, rather than spreading them across support heroes or PvP-only units. The game's recommended charms are somewhat generalized; following them for PvP modes like Arena and Dream Realm will leave a significant amount of potential on the table.

## Dream Realm Top Priorities

### Gerda (Highest Single Impact)
- Usage rate in Dream Realm: essentially 100%, appearing in top formations for all eight bosses.
- Recommended charm set: **Blast**
- Blast charms synergize perfectly with her kit, maximizing the damage of her ramping ultimate and burst phases.
- Verdict: the single most impactful charm investment available for Dream Realm score.

### Shemira (Highest Overall Priority for Refining)
- Remains essential in most top-tier boss compositions.
- Refining her charms dramatically increases both her personal damage and overall Dream Realm score.
- Recommended charm set: **Butcher** (NOT Blast)
- Warning: do not get baited by Blast charms for Shemira — Butcher is the proven superior choice, significantly amplifying her damage over the course of a fight.
- Butcher set is the clear winner across PvE, AFK Stages, Legend Trial, and most Dream Realm bosses.

### Ludovic
- Currently used in multiple high-ranking Dream Realm teams.
- Recommended: if you already have **Insight** or **Butcher** charms equipped on him, refine them immediately.
- Extremely high refining priority.

## Faction Breakdowns

### Graveborn

**Shemira**
- Priority: top tier
- Charm set: Butcher
- Do not use Blast despite the in-game recommendation.

**Gerda**
- Priority: top tier (100% Dream Realm usage)
- Charm set: Blast

### Maulers

The Maulers have a clear hierarchy when it comes to Mythic Charms, with one hero standing above the rest.

**Silven**
- AFK pushing staple; consistent usage on Dream Realm bosses including the Sarral encounter.
- Recommended charm set: **Gale** (Attack + Attack Speed)
- Attack Speed is the name of the game for Silven — faster attacks mean more frequent procs of his extra blades, translating directly to significantly higher damage output.
- Verdict: do not overthink this one, Gale is the clear winner.

### Lightbearer

Lightbearers are not dominating the Dream Realm meta this season.

**Hugan**
- Sees consistent usage in both standard and Endless Dream Realm difficulties.
- Recommended charm set: **Mystic**
- Mystic charms are the safer, more versatile option; if you only want to invest in one set for him, go Mystic.

### Wilder

Described as "absolutely stacked with essential heroes this season" — the bulk of refining attention is recommended here if resources are limited.

**Cecia**
- Role: premier attack buffer and healer; core component of "No Judicator" Dream Realm compositions.
- Recommended charm set: **Arcane**
- Skill Power increases the potency of the Attack buff she grants to allies; Arcane charms maximize her role as a force multiplier for the entire team.
- Verdict: non-negotiable for Dream Realm.

## General Crafting Guidance

- Start with **Sorcery** first for certain heroes — it is the most consistent and widely applicable set across the vast majority of Dream Realm bosses and general content.
- **Gale** charms (Attack + Attack Speed) yield the highest damage ceiling for attack-speed-scaling heroes; tested extensively on Pioneer servers.
- Do not prioritize Blast for heroes whose kits do not specifically scale with burst/ultimate damage.
- For heroes where you have Insight or Butcher already equipped, refine those before crafting new sets.
- Mystic charms are a safe/versatile fallback for heroes whose optimal set is unclear.
