## Rules[]

### Level and Upgrade Adjustments[]

#### Hero Levels[]

In the Arena, Hero Levels are adjusted according to the baseline level (applied to both offensive and defensive Arena formations):

Hero Levels exceeding 151 are set to 151 (baseline level).

For Hero Levels between the baseline level and 240, 10% will be added to the baseline level, setting their actual level for the Arena. Unlocked skills remain unaffected. Hero Equipment Levels are adjusted to match Hero Levels.

#### Non-Season Mode[]

Season upgrade content is not effective in non-season modes. This includes: Season Equipment Resonance Levels, Season Equipment, Season MAgic Charms, Season Artifacts.

### Basics[]

In the Arena, you can spend tickets to challenge others on the same server.

Defeat other players to gain Arena Points and increase your tier.

You can get tier rewards the first time you reach a new tier.

Rewards are tallied and sent out every Monday at 01:00 (UTC+1). The higher your tier, the greater the rewards.

Players who have more than 3,200 Arena Points will be ranked in the Champion Rankings.

If a player hasn't logged in&#32;&#91;sic&#93; for over 14 days, Chippy and Hammie will take over the defense to fend off any challengers. The previous defense formation will be restored once the player logs back in.

### Champion[]

Players who enter the Champion rankings will get Champion Points and be ranked accordingly. Defeat opponents to improve your ranking.

Rewards are tallied and sent our every day at 01:00 (UTC+1). The higher your tier, the greater the rewards.

## Arena Rankings[]

To advance in tiers, players must defeat opponents in battle. Upon accumulating a specified number of Arena Points, higher tiers are unlocked, enhancing the rewards received. Each newly unlocked tier also grants a First-Time Reward. 

An Arena Ticket is required to initiate an attack. Each player receives Arena Ticket ×5 daily after the reset. Additionally, players can purchase up to Arena Ticket ×5 more using Diamonds, with the cost increasing for each additional ticket purchased. This sets the maximum number of attacks per day at 10.

### Rules[]

Increase your ranking before Friday and try not to become one of the last 100 players in Champion tier, or else you'll need to play a Defensive Match at the weekend.

Players who need to play a defensive match at the weekend will be challenged by 1 player with a lower tier and points on the 2 days of the weekend and will be demoted to Legendary III tier if they lose the Defensive Match. If they win, they will stay in the same tier.

Players can be challenged at any time during this phase. Please set a formation in advance.

## Trivia[]

Arena is one of the first Battle Modes added with the game's release.

Arena is one of the Non-Season Battle Modes, which is permanent.

The total amount of "First Time Reward" across all tiers is: Diamond ×23,395, Temporal Essence ×160, and Twilight Essence ×100.
