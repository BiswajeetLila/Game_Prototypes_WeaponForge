## Overview

**Honor Duel** is a Fair Play PVP game mode where players choose an initial formation (Artifact and heroes) to embark on a series of battles against other players. Players purchase heroes and equipment in the Duel Store to strengthen their formations. The challenge ends after achieving 9 wins or experiencing 3 losses.
