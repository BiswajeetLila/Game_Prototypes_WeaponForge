## Faction List[]

There exist 6 factions which most heroes can be a part of:

Hypogeans

Celestials

Lightbearers

Wilders

Maulers

Graveborn

And there is a special faction which heroes from collabrations are part of:

Dimensionals

## Faction Bonus[]

When there are 3/(3&#43;2)/4/5 heroes from the same faction in the formation, ATK and HP of all deployed heroes &#43;10%/14%/18%/22%.

Celestial and Hypogeans can be considered as heroes from any faction when counting Faction Buffs.

For each Celestial and Hypogean on the formation, all deployed heroes' ATK and HP &#43;1%;

## Faction Strategies[]

 Faction strength relation chart

 
When a hero deals damage to a hero from another faction, one of the following will apply:

Increases 15% damage when the target is weak to the hero's faction.

Decrease 15% damage when the target is strong against the hero's faction.

Faction strengths and weaknesses are as follows:

 Lightbearer is strong against Mauler, and weak against Graveborn.

 Mauler is strong against Wilder, and weak against Lightbearer.

 Wilder is strong against Graveborn, and weak against Mauler.

 Graveborn is strong against Lightbearer, and weak against Wilder.
