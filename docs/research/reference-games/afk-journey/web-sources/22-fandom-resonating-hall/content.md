# Resonating Hall

> Source: https://afk-journey.fandom.com/wiki/Resonating_Hall
> Page ID: 10214 | Revision: 47502 | Type: Game Systems

The **Resonating Hall** is where all obtained Heroes are displayed, along with Artifacts and Equipment. Heroes, Artifacts, and Equipment can be upgraded.

## Resonance Level

Heroes are not leveled individually. Only the 5 chosen Hands of Resonance are leveled and Resonance Level is equal to the lowest leveled. All other heroes match their level to the Resonance Level.

### Hands of Resonance

The 5 Hands of Resonance can be changed with any obtained Heroes at any time. Swapping a Hand of Resonance with a hero of a lower level will swap their levels. Hands of Resonance must be within 10 levels of each other.

### Starter Story

During the Starter Story, Resonance Level can only be leveled up to Level 240 using Gold, Training Manuals, and Hero Essence.

## Resonance Synergy

After reach Resonance Level 240, Resonance Synergy unlocks and requires Hero Essence to level up. Training Manuals can now be recycled into Hero Essence (3000 Training Manuals = 1 Hero Essence). Resonance Synergy increases stats just like Resonance Levels until +60, afterwards there is no effect on combat, and instead provide diamond rewards for increasing its level.

## Artifacts

Powerful spells that can be chosen during battle preparation. They can be leveled up to gain new and stronger effects in the Resonance Hall.

## Equipment

Gear that can be upgraded up to the average Resonance Level. Equipment corresponds to each Class and has 6 unique pieces.
