## Overview[]

Every Hero in the game possesses a distinct Exclusive Equipment with unique effects designed specifically for them. The Exclusive Equipment is unlocked separately for each Hero when they reach the Mythic+ rarity, starting at level 0. Unlocking this weapon grants the Hero a new skill, sometimes significantly enhancing their power.

## Unlocking[]

When a Hero reaches Mythic+, their Exclusive Equipment becomes available for unlocking and enhancement. This weapon grants unique stats customized for that Hero and unlocks a fourth ability in their kit, referred to as an Exclusive Skill (EX).

## Upgrading[]

Exclusive Equipments can be upgraded in the Hero Upgrade screen. Every +5 level unlocks a new ability or enhances an existing one.

The upgrade path follows a structured progression, with each level requiring specific resources.
