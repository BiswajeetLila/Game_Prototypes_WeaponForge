# Supreme Arena

**Group:** Routine / Ranked Matches
**Type:** PvP
**Baseline:** 160
**Season:** true
**Token:** Glory Badge

## Event Information

In the Supreme Arena, you can challenge opponents with higher ranks. Upon victory, you can exchange ranks, steadily climbing the leaderboard to earn rewards through continuous challenges. Additionally, the defense map changes weekly, allowing you to adjust hero formations to adapt and climb the ranks faster.

## Rules

### Basics

1. In the Supreme Arena, you can spend Glory Badges to challenge others of the same district. When your three free Glory Badges are used, you may spend Diamonds in order to obtain up to 12 more.
2. You can challenge opponents with higher ranks. Upon victory, you will exchange ranks.
3. Supreme Arena uses a best-of-three format, with victory being achieved by winning two rounds.
4. The default settlement time is 24:00 UTC, but you can change it to anytime between 12:00 UTC to 24:00 UTC and it will take effect the next day.
5. The defensive map changes every week. The off season occurs on Mondays and Tuesdays, during which no challenges can be issued and no rewards will be distributed, though you can still set up your formation in advance.

### Level Restrictions

- Season Levels are adjusted according to the baseline level (applied to both offensive and defensive formations).
- Season Levels exceeding (###) are set to (###) (baseline level.
- For Season Levels between (### + 1) and (### + 50), 20% is added to the baseline level.
- For Season Levels beyond (### + 51), only a minimal amount is added to the baseline level. (Note: This minimal amount does not scale linearly). Unlocked higher-level skills remain unaffected.
- The baseline Season Level of the Supreme Arena will gradually increase weekly. Hero Equipment Levels are adjusted to match Hero Levels.

### Calculation Time Rules

1. You can set your daily reward calculation time to either **12:00 UTC** or **24:00 UTC**. This can be changed at any time, but will only take effect **the next day**.
2. Reward calculation will happen within a 10-minute window of your selected time. If you selected **12:00 UTC**, your rewards are calculated between **11:50 UTC and 12:00 UTC**. If you selected **24:00 UTC**, your rewards are calculated between **23:50 UTC and 24:00 UTC**.

### Additional Restrictions

- This Battle Mode unlocks after reaching Season AFK Stage 130.
- During the last 30 minutes before your set calculation time, you are only allowed to challenge the same opponent after 15 minutes have passed since the last challenge.

## Ranks and Rewards

Both Daily and Weekly Rewards are available for players based on their numerical rank in the district ranging from 1 to below 8000. When the new season begins, there are Challenger bot players that populate the ranks initially on defense but do not attack and swap ranks with active human players. You earn a set amount of gems as a 1st-time reward dependent on the new personal best in ranking you achieve when defeating a swapping ranks with another player.

## Store

Once Supreme Arena is unlocked, the Season Store becomes available at the Emporium. Inventory refreshes at the beginning of every month, and new seasonal heroes from a previous season are added periodically (typically at the end of every new hero banner) as well as Supreme Points Rewards avatar frames.
