In AFK Journey, hero ascension is central to empowering your characters and improving your gameplay. This process involves collecting multiple hero copies, investing in resources, and reaching higher hero levels to unlock powerful skills and combat advantages. With strategic hero promotion, players can dominate in battles and navigate through challenging content in the game.
In this game, your main task is to collect copies of heroes and then enhance them by boosting the system, thereby increasing their strength, level, skills, and combat effectiveness of the entire team. If you want to make your hero stronger and go further in the journey of the game, then the hero's sublimation is crucial. Promotion is the process of upgrading a hero to a higher level, which provides additional stat bonuses. When you reach Mythic Ascension level, you will also unlock exclusive gear. In AFK Journey, the traditional concept of feed has been abandoned, and the whole sublimation system has become more concise.

What is Ascension in AFK Journey?
In AFK Journey, hero sublimation is the key to improving your strength. There are currently 10 heroes that can be upgraded, in both A-Level and S-level categories, available through Noble Tavern. A-Level heroes are capped at 240, while S-level heroes are also capped at 240, but are more expensive to cultivate. Each hero is unique, and investing in them will increase your fighting power and help you win the game.

Requirements for AFK Journey Hero Ascension
Players must have 25 heroes in the same level to unlock each Paragon level. At Paragon level, heroes no longer receive skill upgrades or new skill unlocks, only stat boosts.
The unlocking function is as follows:
● In Legendary+ Tier, characters gain Hero focus, which may enhance stats or bring new effects.
● Upon reaching Mythic+ level, the character will unlock an exclusive weapon, which comes with a new ability that will become more effective as the weapon level increases.
● At Supreme+ level, characters receive a skill boost that makes one of their skills more powerful.
The basics of Ascension:
● For A-Level characters, to reach the top (Supreme +), you need 1 original hero plus 63 copies of the same hero (64 in total), and 200 faction acorns.
● For an S-level character to advance to Supreme+, one original hero, seven replica heroes (out of a total of eight), and 350 camp acorns are required.
● For special characters like Celestial and Hypogean, boosting them requires 1 original character and 13 repeat characters (14 in total).
Paragon Ladder's rules are as follows:
● To reach Paragon 1, you need 25 characters who have reached the Supreme+ level.
● To advance to Paragon 2, you need 20 characters who are already in Paragon 1.
● For Paragon 3, you need 15 Paragon Level 2 characters.
● To reach Paragon 4, you need 15 Paragon 3 level characters.
How to Get More Sould Sigils and Acorns?
To level up your heros in AFK Journey is very important. And Sould Sigils and Acorns are key things for you in this game. Here, we will give you some ways to gain them.
● Recruitment:
Opening the invitation is a quick way to get Faction Acorns and Hero Soul Sigils. While the amount obtained each time may vary, the key is to be able to customize the hero wish list for these letters. Choose according to the hero priority you want to improve to make sure you get the materials you need. It is important to remember that although invitations are the main way to get these items, it is not recommended to buy them with diamonds. The best strategy is to get these letters for free through game progression.
● The Legend Trial:
This battle mode is a golden way to earn Faction Acorns. Participating in these challenges can significantly increase your collection.
● The Nobel Path:
The Nobel Path is similar to the Battle Pass and offers a range of rewards, including invitations and Faction Acorns. Collect 60 soul stones to create a Hero Soul Sigils. If you're planning on staying in the game for the long haul, consider upgrading to Advanced Nobel Path to unlock more rewards.
● Dream Store:
In the Dream Store, you can buy Epic Hero Soul Sigils. This requires the use of Dream Fragments, which you can get in the Dream Realm battle mode.
● Arena Store:
The Arena Store is a great place for players to get Hero Soul Sigils, especially those epic heroes that are hard to get in the invitations. Shopping here requires arena coins, so try to get higher in the rankings in order to accumulate more coins.
● General Store:
While you might find Faction Acorns and invitations at the grocery store, it's not recommended to use diamonds for consumption here. There are more efficient ways to spend your in-game currency.
Each path offers a different way to collect sublimated materials, so take advantage of these opportunities according to your game plan.
You will find multiple ways to access these basic resources. Once you've gathered enough resources, you can head to Resonating Hall. Here, simply select the hero you want to level up and you'll discover the options to improve them.
Don't forget that each hero belongs to a specific Faction, and they need their Faction Acorns to complete their ascension.
Conclusion
Hero ascension in AFK Journey is the path to greater power and enhanced strategy. By collecting hero copies, gathering resources, and investing in heroes strategically, players can build an unstoppable team. Prioritize your ascension based on team needs, resource availability, and hero potential to maximize success in your journey.
