This list that contains detailed information that how GI can perform on different MediaTek chipset, that recommends for some users can run and planning to buy new device in a low cost. Based on the popular benchmarks application, focused on GPU and my research, I prefer to set graphics settings depends on yourself if you want to maintain frame smooth as 60 or playable as 30.

Depending on the CPU and setting, may be encounter stutter when you start the game or during cutscenes on mid-range, it will happen after changing the graphics settings.

## **List of my preferefed custom graphics setting on MTK chipsets**

**Entry-level chipset**:
• Helio P23/Helio P30 - Lowest, 30 fps, Bloom off, Motion Blur off, and Anti-aliasing off

**Entry to Mid-range chipset**:
• Helio P60/Helio P70 - Lowest, 30 fps, Motion blur off, Anti-aliasing off
• Helio P65/Helio G70 - Lowest or Low, 30 fps, Motion Blur off, Anti-aliasing off
• Helio G80/Helio G85 - Lowest or Low, 30 fps, Motion blur off, Anti-aliasing off

**Mid-range chipset**:
• Helio G90/Helio G90T/Helio G95 
- Lowest or Low, 30/60 fps, Anti-aliasing off
- Medium, 30 fps, Motion blur off, Anti-aliasing off
• Dimensity 700
- Lowest or Low, 30/60 fps, Motion Blur off, Anti-aliasing off
- Medium, 30 fps, Motion Blur off, Anti-aliasing off
• Dimensity 720/Dimensity 800U 
- Low or Medium, 30/60 fps, Anti-aliasing off
- High, 30 fps, Motion Blur off, Anti-aliasing off
• Dimensity 800/Dimensity 820/Dimensity 900
- Low or Medium, 60 fps 
- High, 30/60 fps, Motion Blur off, Anti-aliasing off

**High-end chipset**:
• Dimensity 1000C
- Low or Medium, 60 fps
- High, 30/60 fps, Anti-aliasing off
• Dimensity 1000L/Dimensity 1000/Dimensity 1000+
- High, 60 fps, Anti-aliasing off
• Dimensity 1100 - High 60 fps
• Dimensity 1200 - High 60 fps

***Last updated in this list: May 16, 2021***
*this list, it may change or may not change depends on the monthly patch release notes, including performance enhancements of the game, map expansion in Teyvat, etc.*
*If you have experience decreased frames (also cpu throttling) during long gameplay at high temperature, take a rest or cool down your device. If happened, reduce your graphics settings a bit*
*On Helio G85 and below, you may encounter frame drops during teamfights like Venti's Elemental Burst and other effects in multiple enemies.*
*I added **Dimensity 1100** and **Dimensity 1200** that revealed the true power including [vivo S9](https://www.gsmarena.com/vivo_s9-10767.php) and [realme GT Neo](https://www.gsmarena.com/realme_gt_neo-10812.php).*
*I added **Dimensity 900** that would expect a bit improvement on CPU, GPU and our first featured have **LPDDR5 **but LPDDR4x is still available*

**Note**: This list only included on such chipsets with [ARM Mali GPU](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mali_(GPU)) starting 1st Gen Bifrost architecture and newer. I didn't include some because it had a bug in graphics on IMG PowerVR GPU. See [here](https://www.hoyolab.com/genshin/article/75133) and [here](https://www.hoyolab.com/genshin/article/55862).

**Here is the four bugs in graphics on PowerVR GPUs**:
• No grass
• No frozen water
• No Damage dealth/taken, Elemental Reaction text (such as Wet, Electro-charged, Swirl, etc.)
• Inside of the Stormterror Dvalin Domain/Prologue Archon Quest
