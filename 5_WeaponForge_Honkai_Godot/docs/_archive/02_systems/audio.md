# Audio Direction — Stub

**Status:** Deferred from initial GDD. To be specified pre-mockup phase.

## Scope

Music and sound effect direction for WeaponCraft. Critical to nail before art direction is finalized — audio cues set the emotional tone of every UI interaction.

## Open design questions

- **Music genre**: orchestral fantasy (Hero Wars), bouncy chiptune-fantasy (Wittle Defender, Cookie Run), epic-lite (AFK Journey).
- **Forge moment SFX**: anvil clang, magical shimmer, slot-machine ding — which "tells" the player they made a good craft?
- **Discovery moment SFX**: this is the dopamine moment of the game; needs a *distinct, memorable* audio sting.
- **Ultimate cast SFX**: per-class distinct (Warrior whoosh, Mage boom, Rogue stealth-whisper)?
- **Loop length**: stages are 3–4 min — looping every 30s may grate. Multiple variations per chapter?
- **Boss music**: distinct from normal stage music. How dramatic? AFK Arena leans melodic; Wittle leans hype.

## Recommended approach

Lean **Wittle Defender / Cookie Run aesthetic** — bouncy, accessible, fantasy-flavored. Matches the casual tone we've targeted. Avoid grimdark or overly orchestral (alienates casual audience).
