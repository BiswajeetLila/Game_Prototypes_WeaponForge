# Positioning — Stage A

- **Game in one line:** *Forge functions, run the lane, react in chains.* A 3-hero squad auto-walks a 3-lane corridor; you reshape each hero's attack by socketing Functions, then chain cross-hero Magicka reactions.
- **The ONE hook (differentiator nobody else has):** you don't *pull* heroes — you **forge their functions**; and **cross-hero reaction chains** ("I CAUSED THAT").
- **The foil (genre convention we contrast against):** gacha pulls + one-button auto-battlers that play themselves.

## USP-line candidates
1. "You don't pull heroes. You forge their functions."
2. "Every autobattler plays itself. This one waits for *your* combo."
3. "Drag one function. Watch the whole lane change."
