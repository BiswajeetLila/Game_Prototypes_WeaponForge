Battle Drill is a periodic Guild Battle Mode available to all players in a guild who have cleared AFK Stage 50. You can earn Reputation Treasure and Treasure Keys to unlock rewards. The cumulative performance of your Guild will determine their placement in the Battle Drill Rankings.

## Schedule[]

For Guilds in the Starter Season, they will participate in three rounds of Battle drills. Guilds in Season 1 onwards will participate in six rounds of Battle Drills in each season. Full details including the interactive maps for all 7 battle drill maps can be found in the Current & Past Battle Drills tab. For example, the 🔗 Shadowfrost (Interactive Map) is shown on the right.

## Basic Requirements[]

### Basics[]

Battle Drills Rankings will include all Guilds that participated in Battle Drills and occupied at least a passage or stronghold within a round. Guilds will be ranked according to the number of occupied Boss Strongholds and then sorted according to the total damage dealt to Boss in this round.

A new Battle Drills Rankings will be published when the next round starts.

Should a Guild disband, its ranking will be reserved, yet the details will not be available anymore.

### Rules[]

Battle Drills have two stages: a truce phase and a combat phase. During the truce, you can enter Battle Drills to view the enemy formation and plan your own attacks. During the combat phase, you can challenge the enemies. Combat types include Passage Brawl, Stronghold battles, and Boss battles.

You need to depart from your camp and challenge passages and strongholds linked to the camp

You will need to use Stamina to challenge passages and strongholds. You will earn rewards such as Gold and Treasure Keys. Treasure Keys are used to open the Chest of Valor.

You share the progress in challenging passages and strongholds with other Guild members. Once all passages and strongholds have been captured, you will be able to challenge the final boss.

Hero levels will be affected by the baseline level in the Battle Drills mode. Check the Command Center to view the current baseline level. When a hero's level is below the baseline, they will be automatically upgraded to match the baseline. However, if a hero's level surpasses the baseline, 10% of the excess will be added to the baseline level, setting their actual level for Battle Drills.

After you change your Guild, you can still participate in Battle Drills in the new guild. If you have participated in Battle Drills of your former Guild today, you can participate in the Battle Drills of the new Guild the following day.

### Difficulty[]

There are three modes in Battle Drills: Stout Recruit, Seasoned Challenger, and Restless Warrior.

Defeat the final boss in Stout Recruit to advance to the next mode, Seasoned Challenger.

In Seasoned Challeger mode, you will challenge the Battle Drills again with all buffs, camp skills, and camp levels obtained through the previous mode. Defeat the final boss again to advance to Restless Warrior.

In Restless Warrior mode, you will face a never-ending challenge against a final boss that respawns after you defeat it until the end of the current round of Battle Drills.

### Stamina[]

Upon initial deployment, you start with 10 Stamina.

Stamina recovers by 10 daily at 08:00 (UTC+8), storing up to 30 Stamina.

Camps at Level 90 or above recover an extra 5 Stamina daily, storing up to 45.

Hero Stamina Rules:
Each hero recovers 1 Stamina daily, storing up to 3 Stamina.

Deploying a hero to battle costs 1 Stamina: 1 Hero Stamina and Daily Stamina.

### Season Levels[]

Season Level for the current round of Battle Drills will be adjusted to Level 400. Hero Equipment Levels are adjusted to match Hero Level.

## Guild Loot[]

### Chest of Valor[]

You can use Treasure Keys to unlock the Chest of Valor and receive various rewards, including a chance to obtain Diamonds. Treasure Keys can be obtained from Reputation Treasures or by fighting the guards and bosses in Battle Drills.

Treasure Keys that have not been used before the next round of Battle Drills starts will be converted into rewards and issued via mail.

### Reputation Treasure[]

You can earn points through quests to increase your Reputation Level.

Increasing your Reputation Level will unlock brand new rewards.

All unclaimed rewards will be sent to you via mail after each round of Battle Drills.

Full details of rewards given can be found in the Rewards Tab.

Gained buffs will last until the end of the current round of Battle Drills.

## Change History[]

Released in Version 1.0

Version 1.6.1
Passage brawls removed from the battlefield maps as well as from Reputation Quests. Camp Level rewards and Reputation Level requirements adjusted as a result.

Version 1.4.4

Fixed an issue where damage in Battle Drills was abnormal and caused severe lag during battles.

Version 1.4.1

Added new battlefield: Verdshroom Grove.

Version 1.1.14

Added new battlefield: Desolate Grounds.

Version 1.1.13

Adjusted instance design and unlock nodes, optimizing interface experience, and significantly balancing difficulty and reward values.

Fixed abnormal ranking sorting issues in some cases in Rankings.

Version 1.0.11

Reduced the overall difficulty of battles.

Optimized certain interfaces.

Version 1.0.10

Heroes deployed by the Command Center can now be changed.

Optimized the interface experience.

Version 1.0.9

The enhancement item, Impure Divine Ash, can be obtained through Reputation Treasure.

Added Individual Loot selection. Players can now choose the type of resources, allowing for personalized loot selection.

Adjusted Treasure Key drop rules for a more reasonable output.

Adjusted the Gold rewards.

Bosses in Restless Warrior mode can now be defeated and immediately respawn after death.

Version 1.0.7

Remodeled. This mode will be periodically available as an event and is designed to maintain balanced difficulty using a benchmark level. Both the mode itself and the event rewards had undergone substantial changes.

Version 1.0.4

Adjusted the strength of monsters. Removed the Member Chest level so that players can open chests as soon as they get keys. Combined with other related adjustments, Battle Drills no longer drop Dew Selection Chests. They drop equipment instead. Because of this, the chance of getting various types of rewards from Member Chests was also adjusted accordingly. The collection time for Team Chests has been adjusted from Monday at 20:00 to Monday at 00:00 UTC.

Version 1.0.3

Improved the Assist Team's energy recovery efficiency.

Added Dew Choice Chest to the dropped rewards.

The Arena Stats of Exclusive Weapon no longer took effect in Battle Drills.

Version 1.0.1

Fixed an issue where the damage ranking in the Weekly Battle Report was inconsistent with the damage ranking in the Battle Drills Rankings.

Version 1.0

Battle Drills was released.
