**Stats** include the basic status of a battlesuit and can be found in the Valkyrie > Battlesuit > [Upgrade] menu. These will greatly affect how a Captain's gameplay operates, as they typically calculate how well a Valkyrie will perform on the field.

Although minor, other in-game aspects can also have stats, such as AstralOps/ELFs, playable A Post-Honkai Odyssey/A Post-Honkai Odyssey Chapter 2 characters, and Open World familiars. Additionally, non-playable features can also have stats, like A Post-Honkai Odyssey's enemies, weapons, and stigmata.

## Visible Stats

Visible Stats include the stats that can be seen once in the [Upgrade] or [Stats] menu. For other in-game aspects that have stats, the ones seen once in their respective menus are also included here.

- **Type:** Refers to the name of the stat listed. The type of each stat will typically be listed to the left or right of its numbers.
  - **Health Points (HP):** Refers to the max amount of health a Valkyrie has, meaning how much damage she can take before being killed. In descriptions, **Max HP** refers to the total stat number and not any effects in battle that may change it.
  - **Energy Points[*citation needed*] (SP):** Refers to the maximum amount of SP a Valkyrie has, which can be used for Weapon Skills and Ultimates when in battle.
  - **Attack (ATK):** Refers to the maximum amount of ATK a Valkyrie has. ATK is usually one of the most impactful stats for battle, as it calculates how much damage a Valkyrie will do. Damage Calculation, or ATK calculation, is often needed because the maximum amount of ATK listed is changed when in battle due to a wide variety of skills.
  - **Defense (DEF):** Refers to the maximum amount of how much a Valkyrie can reduce the damage of an attack. DEF can only block Physical DMG, not Elemental DMG. The higher a Valkyrie's maximum DEF, the less damage they will take.
  - **Critical Points (CRT):** Refers to a *part* of a calculation for a Valkyrie's Critical Rate. This means that the CRT a Valkyrie has does not reflect her actual Crit Rate. Additionally, the amount of CRT a Valkyrie has can only be used if said Valkyrie deals Physical DMG, as Elemental DMG cannot Crit.
  - **DMG Type:** Only seen in ELFs, this refers to the type of damage an ELF does in combat. The DMG Type of a Valkyrie, AstralOp, weapon, etc. will already be listed as a core feature of theirs.
- **Rank:** Refers to the current rank (typically B~SSS) of a Valkyrie. When a Valkyrie's rank goes up, she will gain certain [Bonus Stats] and may unlock new skills.
  - **Star Level:** Refers to the current star level (typically 1★~4★) of an ELF. When an ELF's star level goes up, she will gain certain [Bonus Stats] and may unlock new skills.
  - **Rank Up:** Refers to the current rank (typically S~SSS) of an AstralOp. When an AstralOp's rank goes up, she will gain certain [Bonus Stats] and unlock new skills.
- **Fragments/Souls:** Refers to the amount of Fragments/Souls that are required to evolve a Valkyrie's rank.
  - **Parts:** Refers to the amount of Parts that are required to evolve an ELF's star level.
- **Experience (EXP):** Refers to the Character EXP a Valkyrie has or needs to level up. Every level rewards upgraded stats and may unlock new skills.
- **Load:** Refers to the Gear load, or current equipment of a Valkyrie. If the current gear load exceeds the max gear load, then no more gears can be equipped.

## Invisible Stats

Invisible Stats are stats that don't directly show up in any menus and typically need to be calculated by the Captain.

### Critical Rate/Critical Chance (CRR)/Crit Rate

*Main article: *Damage Calculation#Critical Strike Chance
Critical Rate refers to the chance of hitting a crit and dealing Crit DMG. For more information, see the Damage Calculation article. Despite being an invisible stat, Critical Rate can be affected by direct buffs from Valkyrie skills, stigmata, weapons, etc.

### Attack Speed (ATK SPD)

Attack Speed is a special modifier that affects the overall DPS (damage-per-second) of a Valkyrie by increasing how fast she attacks.

- All Valkyries have ATK SPD by default.
- Not all attacks will benefit from this stat ([img:Herrscher of Reason (Icon)] Herrscher of Reason's Charged Attacks, for example).
- +100% ATK SPD means the character will do attacks ~2x faster if not affected by hitlag and fully gain benefit from this stat.
- Bronya's Charged Attacks have a soft-cap when ATK SPD is beyond 200%.

### Other Stats

- Physical Resistance / Elemental Resistance / All-damage Resistance.
- **Shield Damage Multiplier (SDM):** Refers to the amount of damage a Valkyrie does to shields, and is +1 by default. Every bit of damage can be added to the +1.
