## Hero Equipment

Hero Equipment can be purchased at the Duel Store and equipped on any hero. Each hero can wear one piece of Equipment, which can be removed or replaced as the player sees fit. Equipment can also be obtained through upgrading Honor Duel artifacts to 2 stars or as a random reward from Fitz's Trials.

Equipment comes in four rarities: Rare, Elite, Epic, and Legendary. Rare equipment can be purchased for 10 Honor Badges, Elite equipment can be purchased for 20 Honor Badges, and Epic equipment can be purchased for 40 Honor Badges. Legendary equipment can only be received as a reward for levelling up Honor Duel artifacts to 2 stars.

Honor Duel equipment cannot be sold.
