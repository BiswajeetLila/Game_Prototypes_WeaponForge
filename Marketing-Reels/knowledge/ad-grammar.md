# Ad grammar (canonical seed — enrich + longevity-validate via LEARN)

> Seeded from general mobile-UA knowledge. Treat as **hypotheses** until confirmed against longevity data in `pattern-library/<genre>.md`. Mark each confirmed pattern with its evidence (creatives + run-length).

## 1. Hook archetypes (first 1–3 seconds — the thumbstop)
| Hook | What it does | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| Payoff-first | Open cold on the dopamine moment (the big combo/win) | mechanic-driven games |
| Problem / POV | "POV: every X bores you in 10s" — name the pain | differentiated games vs a tired genre |
| UGC / hand-cam | Real hand on phone, genuine reaction | trust, authenticity, TikTok-native |
| Fake-fail / rage-bait | Player does it "wrong" → viewer wants to correct | puzzle/strategy |
| Oddly-satisfying / ASMR | Sensory loop (fill, merge, slice) | casual, merge, craft |
| Stat blitz | Rapid numbers ("3 heroes · 12 funcs · 15 reactions") | depth/build games |
| Character reaction | Face/cam reacts to on-screen event | personality-led |
| "Wait for it" / delayed payoff | Tension → release | retention past 3s |
| Choice / fork | "Which would you pick?" | decision-driven games |

## 2. Micro-structures (the ~15s body)
- **Hook → retain → CTA** (default): 0–1s stop, 1–12s escalate/prove, 12–15s call.
- **Problem → solution → proof**: pain, your mechanic, the result.
- **Seamless loop**: ends where it starts → replays invisibly (boosts watch-time).
- Rule: one idea per creative. Don't explain the whole game.

## 3. Mobile-game ad archetypes (genre playbook — confirm relevance per game)
Fake-gameplay/idealized · "this is actually a [genre]" bait-switch · satisfying-merge/fill · save-the-character (pull-pin) · build/craft ASMR · power-fantasy escalation. *Use for structure/anti-pattern awareness; lead with YOUR differentiator, don't clone.*

## 4. Sound-off design (most feeds autoplay muted)
- The first frame must read with **zero audio**: burned-in caption, a face, or motion.
- Caption every beat; keep text in the safe zone (avoid platform UI overlap top/bottom ~15%).
- Audio is a bonus layer, never load-bearing.

## 5. Anti-patterns (kill on sight)
- Logo/studio-sting open (wastes the 1s that matters).
- Explaining mechanics before showing a payoff.
- Cloning a saturated competitor hook (you arrive late + buried + your edge is hidden).
- Aspirational footage a prototype can't deliver, unlabeled (validates a mirage).
