# Retention Arc — Day-in-the-Life (D1 → D20)

**Date:** 2026-06-12 · **Purpose:** the *narrative of a retained player* for the CEO greenlight pitch. The [design spec](2026-06-11-hero-squad-meta-design.md) has the *systems*; this has the *felt experience* + the hook pulling the player back each day.

**Beacon user:** the **craft-tinkerer** (Brotato / Backpack Hero / Potion Craft lineage — comes for build-craft depth, gacha-resistant). Provisional; revisit.

**Funnel spine (the one sentence):** **Come for the craft, stay for the discovery, monetize via the collection.**
- *Craft* acquires + hooks (minute 1).
- *Discovery* (recipe codex, counter-build) retains the engaged player (the moat).
- *Collection* (heroes: level / star / bond / signature weapons) is the long-game goal ladder — and where money lives.

## The arc

| When | What the player DOES | What they FEEL | The hook back | Systems (phase) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **D1 · sessions 1–3** | Craft a 3-slot weapon in the shop, fight wave 1, hit a first **recipe discovery** (🔥+❄ = Steamburst). Mid-FTUE, a **scripted hero pull** lands — "NEW HERO!". Squad forms (2 heroes). Mastery of the system, not a grind. | "Beat the stage that beat me; push my main hero higher." | stage-affinity telegraph (P1) · boss retry (exists) · Hero Level (P0) |
| **D7** | Roster ~3–5 heroes. Recipe codex half-filled. First **duplicate pulls → Star-up** begins. Player has a *main* and a preferred build identity. | Ownership. "*My* squad, *my* go-to build." | **D14** | **Mastery/bond** milestones unlock dialogue + **portrait evolution** on the favorite hero. **Permanent knowledge** — a mastered recipe becomes a favorited blueprint, faster to rebuild. Crafting feels *deeper*, not repetitive — knowledge compounds. | "Bond my favorite to the next tier; unlock her signature path." | Mastery + portrait evolution + permanent knowledge (P2) |

Long-term goals visible. | "Push Elite/Nightmare; chase the new monthly hero; the season reset." | signature weapons + squad synergy (P3) · seasons + difficulty tiers (live-ops) |

## Where the two kill-shots show up in the arc

The CEO review found two ways this dies. The arc tells you *when* each would surface — which is exactly when to be measuring:

1. **Audience mismatch** (craft fan won't touch gacha) → surfaces at **D7**, the moment the collection ladder is supposed to take over from pure craft novelty. *Watch:* does the craft-engaged player make a pull, or churn at the gacha wall? The **P1 monetization probe sits exactly here.**
2. **Moat erosion** (heroes outshine craft) → surfaces **D14–D20**, as hero power (Star/Mastery/signature) compounds. *Watch:* is the player still making meaningful *craft* decisions, or auto-winning on hero power? The deferred **moat guardrail** (craft ≥60% invariant) is the fix if it bites.
