**Resonance Level** in the Gameplay Guide. It is acquired upon entering the Resonating Hall.

## Content

**Resonance Level**

---

The lowest-level memeber of Hands of Resonance sets the **Resonance Level**. Resonance heroes will automatically raise in Resonance Level.

When changing members of Hands of Resonance, their hero levels will also be **swapped**.
