## 1. Style cohesion rules (v1.1)

1. **Stylized 2.5D cel-shaded** with bold line work, slightly stylized proportions (NOT chibi, NOT super-deformed, NOT photoreal-anime). Casual-mobile-RPG register — closer to **Archero / Wittle Defender** than Honkai Star Rail. ~5-6 head proportions.
2. **Enemies smaller-scale for silhouette** — NEVER cute-mascot. Bosses (Slime King, Iron Lich's Herald) full-scale dramatic.
3. **NO chibi, NO super-deformed, NO sticker-style, NO kawaii-mascot.**
4. **Named assets inline** — "Stormblaze Katana", "Iron Lich's Herald", "FIRESTORM Catalyst", "Master Smith's Forge", "Frostbite Wand" — call them out specifically.
5. **16:9 cinematic** for story-beat key-frames (these are pitch-deck framings, not screen-states).
6. **Cast descriptors** (§2) copy-pasted verbatim — never paraphrased.
7. **Dialogue caption optional** — story beats with a memorable line (SB5 Paladin, SB6 squad-talk, SB9 verdict) can bake a clean caption into a bottom letterbox bar.
