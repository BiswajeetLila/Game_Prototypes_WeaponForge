# WeaponCraft — D1 Gameplay Video / Animatic Script (text-only)

**Purpose:** beat-sliced shot list for the 30–90s gameplay/marketing video (concept §9.3). Maps each shot to a rendered 2E mockup frame so this can be storyboarded or cut as an animatic. Art style locked = **2E (cel-shaded 3D anime)**. Target cut = **~60s**.

**Frame source:** `docs/research/d1-beat-mockups/2e-beats/`

**Cast / continuity:** Bran (warrior, white spiky hair, dark teal-trim armor, fire-storm katana) · Elara (mage, silver hair, blue-teal robe, cyan staff) · Vex (rogue, dark hood, twin daggers) · Hot Paladin (warrior-priestess, storm-blue greatsword) · Master Smith (Stage 10).

Legend — each shot: **[timecode] FRAME → action / CAMERA / VFX / SFX+MUSIC / ON-SCREEN TEXT**

---

## COLD OPEN — "Hold the line" (0:00–0:04)

- **[0:00–0:02]** Black → studio logo sting.
- **[0:02–0:04]** Dim forge chamber. Bran raises the fire-storm katana toward camera, embers drifting.
  - CAMERA: slow push-in on Bran, low angle.
  - VFX: ember particles, rim-light flare off the blade.
  - SFX/MUSIC: anvil *clang* on logo → low war-drum swell.
  - TEXT: *"Hold the line."* fades in/out. Tap-to-start prompt pulse.

---

## BEAT 1 — Core combat read (0:04–0:12) · FRAME `beat1-combat-read`

- **[0:04–0:07]** Cut to side-view arena. Squad of 3 holds the LEFT; green slimes pour from the RIGHT. Heroes auto-attack, damage numbers pop (450 / 720 / 330), slimes burst into cyan XP cubes.
  - CAMERA: locked gameplay framing (the screenshot), tiny idle parallax on the background lava.
  - VFX: katana fire-trail, Elara ice shimmer, XP-cube burst.
  - SFX/MUSIC: rhythmic hit *thwacks*, slime *splats*, light battle loop kicks in.
  - TEXT: HUD live — **WAVE 1**, gold 3500, gems 250, 3 portrait ult-rings filling.
- **[0:07–0:12]** Finger-tap pip lands on a hero portrait → quick ult flash to show "tap = ultimate."
  - TEXT (caption): *"Auto-battle. Tap to unleash."*

---

## BEAT 2 — Forge Draft transform / the "whoa" (0:12–0:22) · FRAME `beat2-forge-draft`

- **[0:12–0:15]** Wave clears → FORGE DRAFT modal slides up over the lower half. Three cards: **+20% ATK SPEED**, **+200% HP**, **STORM CYCLONE ×3** (glowing, tooltip arrow).
  - CAMERA: modal slide-up ease; subtle card-hover bob.
  - SFX: card *whoosh* + UI chime.
  - TEXT: **WAVE 2** banner; card labels.
- **[0:15–0:22]** Finger taps the ×3 card → cut back to arena → Bran's next swing spawns **three** storm cyclones instead of one. Slimes scatter.
  - CAMERA: quick punch-in on the triple-cyclone, then pull back.
  - VFX: 1 cyclone → 3, electric-blue swirl, screen-shake on impact.
  - SFX/MUSIC: rising "power-up" sweetener; music adds a layer.
  - TEXT (caption): *"Draft cards that rewrite your abilities."*

---

## BEAT 4 — Forge Wheel pull / the USP (0:22–0:34) · META screen · FRAME `beat4-forge-wheel-pull`

- **[0:22–0:25]** Cut to the FORGE WHEEL meta screen (out of gameplay) — anvil + gear motif, single tall reel spinning, sparks.
  - CAMERA: push toward the reel; speed-lines.
  - SFX/MUSIC: slot-reel *ratchet* loop, tension hold, music drops to a held note.
- **[0:25–0:30]** Reel slams down → crescendo → lands on **STORMBLAZE KATANA — Warrior — Fire-imbued** in a gold legendary frame, light-rays.
  - CAMERA: jackpot flash-zoom, confetti.
  - VFX: legendary god-rays, ember confetti.
  - SFX/MUSIC: jackpot *chime* + bass hit; music re-enters triumphant.
- **[0:30–0:34]** Bran's bust portrait reacts (proud stoic smile). Banner: *"Bran wields the Stormblaze Katana."*
  - TEXT (caption): **"You don't pull heroes. You forge their weapons."** ← key differentiator line.

---

## BEAT 3 — Boss DEFEAT / Iron Lich's Herald (0:34–0:45) · gameplay, hardcoded loss · FRAME `beat3-boss-defeat`

- **[0:34–0:37]** Back in gameplay. Boss banner slams in: the towering **IRON LICH'S HERALD** (dark wraith-lich, cold blue-black energy) fills the RIGHT; red boss HP bar near full.
  - CAMERA: banner wipe, slight zoom-out to fit the boss.
  - SFX/MUSIC: ominous boss roar; music → dark boss theme.
- **[0:37–0:42]** A couple of quick exchanges, then the Herald slams a wave of dark energy → the squad is overwhelmed; Bran drops to a knee, Elara thrown, Vex reeling. Boss HP barely dents.
  - CAMERA: shove-back shake, then slow pull-back.
  - VFX: blue-black energy shockwave, impact dust; hero ult-rings flicker out; red low-HP vignette.
  - SFX/MUSIC: heavy thuds; music darkens.
- **[0:42–0:45]** Heroes down on the cracked ground; the Herald looms. No victory — a hard, scripted loss.
  - TEXT (caption): *"Some threats are beyond one squad."*

---

## BEAT 5 — Hot Paladin rescue / session-2 hook (0:45–0:58) · cinematic · FRAME `beat5-hot-paladin-cinematic`

- **[0:45–0:49]** Cinematic letterbox in. Aftermath of the loss: the squad is slumped on the cracked ground, **dejected** (Bran propped on his planted sword head low, Elara on her knees, Vex against rubble); the **Iron Lich's Herald** shadow recedes behind them.
  - CAMERA: low, still; faint warm break on the horizon.
  - SFX/MUSIC: near-silence + quiet wind, somber low.
- **[0:49–0:55]** The **HOT PALADIN** strides in at GROUND LEVEL through the smoke and plants her glowing storm-blue greatsword; warm light spreads, her blade-glow catches the heroes' faces — a grounded ray of hope (no divine beam, no sky descent).
  - CAMERA: slow push-in toward the Paladin.
  - VFX: dust swirl, gentle blade glow, embers, warm dawn breaking through the haze.
  - SFX/MUSIC: warm music swell + blade hum.
- **[0:55–0:58]** She extends a hand to the fallen Bran; he lifts his head, the squad stirs. Roster slot 4 lights up (3 lit + shadows behind).
  - TEXT: dialogue *"I saw your blades work hard. Let me join."* · caption: *"Seven heroes. Each with a story."*
