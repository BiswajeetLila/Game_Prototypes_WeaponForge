# WeaponCraft — Style & Theme Bible

**Anchor (visual truth):** `_art-build/ref-gameplay.png` (hosted: `https://i.ibb.co/zhz3TFPq/Gemini-Generated-Image-dhm62ndhm62ndhm6.png`). On any conflict between words and image, **the image wins.**

## Theme
Cozy heroic fantasy. A small band of chibi adventurers craft weapons at an anvil and battle cute cartoon monsters. Warm, charming, light-hearted — **never grimdark, never photoreal.**

## Art style & render
Hand-painted 2D, soft cel-shading with painterly rendering. Chibi-heroic proportions (~3–3.5 heads tall), big expressive eyes, thick clean silhouettes. Warm top-left key light + soft rim light, gentle gradients, soft drop shadows. Register: **Cookie Run: Kingdom × Wittle Defender × AFK Journey.**

## Palette
- **Base UI:** warm wood / parchment — walnut brown, tan, cream. Dark-brown dividers + battle-log bar.
- **Primary CTA:** leafy green (START WAVE / Battle).
- **Recipe accent:** violet/purple pills.
- **Element pops:** fire orange-red · ice cyan-blue · nature/earth green · lightning yellow · pierce steel.
- **Economy:** gold coins.

## Lighting
Warm, soft, cozy torch-lit forge glow. Actives glow (recipe pills, ULT bars, crit numbers). No harsh shadows.

## Camera
Flat front-facing 2D, **vertical 9:16 portrait.** UI-first composition: HUD framing dominates; combat sits in a shallow side-view stage band across the top third. (Key art is the only landscape, cinematic exception.)

## Key subjects (OUR roster — NOT the ref's Leo/Elara/Zara)
- **Bran** — boy warrior, chestnut tousled hair, freckles, green tunic + brown leather pauldrons, short sword.
- **Elara** — ice mage, long silver-blue hair, hooded frost-blue robe, glowing crystal staff, cyan accent.
- **Vex** — rogue, short violet hair, dark hooded leather, twin curved daggers, violet accent.
- **Enemies:** green slime, goblin, skeleton (cute, cartoon, not scary). Bosses (later): slime king, iron golem, arcane lich.
- **Parts:** framed icon cards — Head (helm/visor), Hilt (grip/handle), Rune (glowing element gem).

## UI / HUD language
Wood-framed rounded panels. Hero cards = portrait + green HP bar + orange ULT bar. Part cards = rounded, element-tinted, tier rim, green `+ATK` text. Recipe pills = violet rounded chips + small effect icon. Big chunky green CTA. Gold coin badge. Shop = row of element-tinted gem/part cards. Floating damage numbers: chunky, yellow for crit, white for normal, element-tinted on element hits. Dark battle-log bar with one line.

## Typography
Rounded friendly bold sans for numbers/labels (cream/white with dark outline). Small-caps slot labels (`HEAD / HILT / RUNE`). Chunky button text.

## Do / Don't
- **Do:** cozy, readable, juicy; ONE consistent game; real functional HUD; our roster; ref's exact palette + framing.
- **Don't:** grimdark / photoreal; banners, titles, logos, or marketing/promo words on gameplay mockups; clutter; off-palette neon; the ref's hero names.
