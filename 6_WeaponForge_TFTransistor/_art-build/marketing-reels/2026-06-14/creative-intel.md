# Creative intel — Stage B (run 2026-06-14)

> Source: LEARN phase (`Marketing-Reels/knowledge/pattern-library/ios-games-baseline.md`). 20 longest-running iOS-Games creatives torn down (Applovin + Unity). **Caveat:** broad iOS-Games category, not tactical-autobattler-specific (genre-targeted re-pull is TODO).

## Format / saturation
- **Length:** 30–60s dominates; 112–119s "playable-style" funnels exist for match-3.
- **Format:** vertical 720×1280, sound-off, captioned, rewarded/interstitial.
- **Oversaturated → AVOID cloning:** pull-the-pin / save-the-character fake-fail (Dream Games + Toon Blast + Color Block Jam all run it — high imitation = fatigue; we'd arrive late).
- **Networks with coverage:** Applovin, Unity (Instagram + TikTok = 0 coverage in this provider's window).

## The durable winning grammar (longevity-weighted)
1. **Engineered hook, NOT a gameplay demo** — virtually no long-runner opens on raw gameplay.
2. **#1 longevity family = oddly-satisfying / ASMR cascade** (Word Search ≥1056d, Vita Mahjong 486d). "I want to finish that."
3. Pull-the-pin fake-fail = proven but saturated. IQ-challenge ("are you smart enough") = durable non-rage-bait lane. Power-fantasy escalation = instant dopamine.

## Mapping to OUR differentiator (forge functions + cross-hero reaction chains)
| Winning pattern | Our authentic version |
|---|---|
| ASMR satisfying-cascade (#1 longevity) | the **cross-lane reaction chain** detonating (Steam/Electrocute), counter spiking — genuinely satisfying, genuinely ours |
| Choice fake-fail (saturated) | "which function do you socket?" — wrong pick → squad overwhelmed → right forge → chain saves it (adapt, don't clone) |
| Save-the-character peril | heroes about to be overrun → you forge the function just in time |
| "Are you smart enough" build-IQ | "most players socket FIRE here; pros put it in the modifier" — for the build-depth cohort |

## What to open on (first 1s)
The **reaction-chain payoff** — it's the #1 winning family AND our actual differentiator. Lead there; don't bury it behind a logo or a slow setup.

## Anti-mimicry mandate
≥1 hook variant must lead with the differentiator (forge/chain). Do NOT lead with a generic pull-the-pin clone — it's saturated and hides our edge.
