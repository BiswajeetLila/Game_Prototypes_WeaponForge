## Additional DMG

### Type

Combat
Causes the target being hit to take extra DMG, which is not considered an attack.
**Additional DMG** is a type of damage dealt through certain abilities. Additional DMG is *not* considered an attack. In combat, Additional DMG is displayed as individual damage numbers separated from other damage sources.

There are two main sources of Additional DMG: Character's abilities, in which case it's calculated similarly to regular damage, and Weakness Break, in which case it's calculated similarly to Break DMG.

Character Additional DMG can score CRIT hits and is affected by Element/All-type DMG Boost. However, it is *not* affected by DMG Boost that is limited to certain DMG tags (e.g. Basic Attack, Skill, Ultimate, Follow-Up Attack, DoT, etc.) unless specified.

Ice's [img:Icon Frozen] **Frozen** and Quantum's [img:Icon Entanglement] **Entanglement** deal Weakness Break Additional DMG at the start of the target's turn. Additional DMG of Frozen and Entanglement triggered by Weakness Break cannot score CRIT hits and is *not* affected by DMG Boost.

Abilities that "additionally deal DMG" (adds Extra DMG to Base DMG calculation) are not to be confused with Additional DMG (capitalized and underlined in description).
