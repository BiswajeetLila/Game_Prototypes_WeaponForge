# Soul Pact

**Soul Pact** is a Seasonal Mechanic introduced in Thorns of Devotion.

## Rules

1. Soul Pact is featured in **selected game modes** during the Thorns of Devotion, Tower of Memory and Crown of Ashes seasons. This feature allows Magisters to deploy **3** heroes from the same faction to activate that faction's Phantimal. Deploying **3** Celestial or Hypogean heroes will activate the Celestial & Hypogean Faction Phantimal.
2. During the preparation phase, Magisters can adjust the positions of the active Phantimal. Once battle begins, the active Phantimal will **fight alongside the player**.
3. After upgrading a Phantimal to a certain level, it can designate an ally behind it as the **spirit-marked hero**, granting them powerful bonuses in battle.
4. A Phantimal's stats are determined by the **levels and tiers** of the deployed heroes.
5. In game modes where Soul Pact is active, the player activated Phantimals will gain bonus ATK and HP depending on how many Dimensional heroes they have on the battlefield.

## Phantimal Upgrades & Ascensions

1. Magisters can use upgrade materials to level up their Phantimals, enhancing their stats and skills. Phantimals' upgrade progress **resets** at the end of each season.
2. Resetting a Phantimal returns all materials used. Players can reset Phantimals for free once every **3 days** or use Phantimal Reshapers when a free reset is unavailable.
