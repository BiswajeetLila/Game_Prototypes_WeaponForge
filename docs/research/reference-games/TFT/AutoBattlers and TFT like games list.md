# AutoBattlers and TFT like games list


## Roguelike & Single-Player Auto Battlers

1. **Vivid Knight**
You collect characters trapped in jewels, combine three identical ones to upgrade them, and build teams based on shared symbols (synergies) to clear roguelike dungeons.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch 15-Minute Tutorial & First Dungeon Gameplay](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1YwK15i3js4)


2. **Despot's Game: Dystopian Army**
A roguelike tactical auto-battler where you equip a squad of puny humans with weapons that define their class (Cultist, Ninja, Mage). Matching classes unlocks TFT-style synergy bonuses as you fight through a mutant-filled labyrinth.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch Video Review & Gameplay](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dZuPZFEMlRs)


3. **SNKRX**
A unique arcade roguelite where you control a snake made of auto-battler heroes. You buy units from a shop, combine duplicates to level them up, and build class synergies to survive waves of enemies.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch Overview, Gameplay & Impressions](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-_o5zPPIBI8)


4. **King God Castle**
A brilliant hybrid of base defense and auto-battler. You use silver to summon random heroes onto a grid, merge identical ones to increase their tier, and position them to defend against incoming PvE waves.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch Story Mode & Battle Strategy](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2yfKx3Yup2M)


5. **Tavern Rumble**
A strategy roguelike card game that utilizes auto-chess combat. You draft units from different factions, place them on a tactical board, and watch them fight automatically based on their placement and synergies.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch Android Gameplay Walkthrough](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rAWnGRrSXFQ)


6. **Loop Hero**
While different from a standard TFT draft, it scratches the exact same strategic itch. Instead of controlling the hero, you draft the environment, enemies, and loot, watching the auto-battles play out and optimizing your "deck" of tiles.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch Official Mobile Gameplay](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oiVP950LgqY)


7. **Merge Tactics**
A grid-based kingdom defense game where you draft and merge blocks (units) mid-battle. Upgrading units and placing them strategically to maximize their auto-attacking capabilities is the core gameplay loop.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch High-Level Strategy & Gameplay](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9j503NfkQRI)



## Strategy RPGs with Auto-Chess Combat

8. **Brave Nine**
A highly tactical RPG where you draft mercenaries, arrange them on a grid, and set their attack order. Once the battle starts, it plays out automatically. The massive PvE campaign requires deep positional strategy to clear.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch Official Gameplay Showcase](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLANJ8LsVMNnk6DOGpDNCEFFhUBVWkzh9n)


9. **Mythic Legends**
Draft champions, manage your economy, and build synergies. While it pits you against other players' boards, the multiplayer is entirely asynchronous, meaning you play at your own pace through a PvE-style campaign progression.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch Official Release Gameplay](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BWdkh7GniL8)


10. **Warcraft Rumble**
A PvE-heavy action strategy game where you draft a team of Warcraft miniatures. You deploy them on map lanes, and they auto-path and auto-battle toward the boss. Faction synergies and unit counters are critical.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch 4K Gameplay Walkthrough](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLDCf3CtN2AKJy-duL73zKQlmfyVyYmFP7)


11. **Auto Brawl Chess**
This game offers the traditional 8-player PvP auto-chess experience, but stands out by including a dedicated, fully fleshed-out PvE campaign mode where you solve auto-battler puzzles and clear linear stages.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch iOS/Android Gameplay Walkthrough](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2dTZefloIZk)



## Idle & AFK Auto Battlers

12. **AFK Journey**
A beautifully animated RPG where combat takes place on a hex grid. You draft heroes, build faction synergies, and must position them carefully around obstacles before the auto-battle begins.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch Full Gameplay First Look](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3zdOPANThe4)


13. **AFK Arena**
The predecessor to AFK Journey. It uses a 5v5 auto-battler format where placing the right unit in the right slot (frontline vs. backline) and matching faction bonuses is the primary way to beat the massive PvE campaign.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch Event and Tier List Gameplay](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M9hUzMla-UA)


14. **Eversoul**
An idle RPG heavily focused on team composition. You collect characters, arrange them in specific tactical formations, and utilize type-advantages to clear PvE stages automatically.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch Official Skill Animations and Gameplay](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/THu-KYiUxZs)


15. **Mobile Legends: Adventure**
An idle auto-battler spin-off of the popular MOBA. It features a massive PvE story mode where you collect heroes, match their synergies, and upgrade them to push through increasingly difficult auto-battles.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Explore the Official Gameplay Channel](https://www.youtube.com/c/MobileLegendsAdventure/videos)


16. **Idle Heroes**
One of the oldest games in the genre, featuring a massive roster of heroes. Success in the PvE campaign comes entirely from understanding the game's complex synergy web and positioning your units correctly before combat.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch End-Game Event Gameplay](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pYcy0FDmxF0)


17. **Watcher of Realms**
A hybrid of tower defense and auto-battler. You draft heroes with specific classes, place them on a grid to defend against waves, and manage their synergies as they auto-attack enemies.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch New Hero Breakdown & Gameplay](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5B_X5JZVQyg)



## Auto-Chess Games with Dedicated PvE Modes

18. **Teamfight Tactics (Tocker's Trials Mode)**
While primarily the king of PvP, TFT recently introduced "Tocker's Trials," an official, permanent PvE game mode where you play solo against increasingly difficult waves of bots and unique bosses without round timers.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch Tocker's Trials Boss Fights & Strategies](https://www.youtube.com/hashtag/tockerstrials)


19. **Dota Underlords (City Crawl Mode)**
Valve's auto-battler features an excellent single-player "City Crawl" campaign mode. You solve board-state puzzles, fight specific PvE challenges, and can play full matches against advanced AI bots at your own pace.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch a Complete Beginner's Walkthrough](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cq37Y9ls4_I)


20. **Super Auto Pets**
An async auto-battler where you draft cute animals with unique abilities and merge them to level up. Because you only play against "ghosts" of other players' teams and there is no turn timer, it feels entirely like a relaxing, solo PvE puzzle.
* **Gameplay Link:** [Watch a High-Level Challenge Run](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ceDyGw7FHlU)
