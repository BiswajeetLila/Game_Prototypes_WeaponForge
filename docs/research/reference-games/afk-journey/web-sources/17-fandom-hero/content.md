# Hero

> Page ID: 5064 | Type: Game Systems | Status: Stub

**Heroes** are obtainable units in *AFK Journey*. They come in different rarities, belong to a Faction, categorized into a Class, and can wield Exclusive Equipment.

## How to Obtain

Heroes are primarily obtained via Recruitment using Invite Letters, Epic Invite Letters, Rate Up Invite Letters, or Stellar Crystals. These Recruitment Items can be:

- Earned from Events and Quests.
- Purchased in the Emporium with various items.
- Purchased with real money in the Trolley or Limited Bundles.

Other ways to obtain characters include:

- 1st Purchase Reward
  - Rowan can be obtained as part of the first purchase rewards.
- Quests
  - Chippy and Hammie are obtained at the start.
- Guarantee
  - Cecia is a guaranteed character on the 10th recruit. One of Smokey & Meerky, Hewynn, or Rowan is chosen at random to be a guaranteed character on the 30th recruit.
- Selector Chests
  - Rare chests offer a selection of heroes where one can be chosen and obtained.
- A-Level Soulstones and S-Level Soulstones.
  - Obtain a random A-Level or S-Level hero, respectively.
- Events
- Arena Store
  - A selection of S-Level heroes can be purchased with Arena Coins with a monthly limit on each.
- Dream Store
  - A selection of A-Level heroes can be purchased with Dream Fragments with a monthly limit on each.
- Guild Store
  - Celestial and Hypogean heroes can be purchased with Guild Medals with a monthly limit on each.
- Season Store
  - Heroes from previous Seasons, excluding Celestial and Hypogean heroes, can be purchased with Season Tokens with a monthly limit on each.

## Progression

### Leveling

*See also: Resonating Hall*

Heroes are initially leveled individually. Completing the tutorial unlocks Resonance Levels where 5 heroes are selected as the Hands of Resonance and all other heroes match their lowest level. Only the Hands of Resonance can be leveled and Hands of Resonance must be within 10 levels of each other.

Heroes gain levels using Gold, Training Manuals, and Hero Essence from Level 1 to Level 240. After unlocking Season Story, heroes can be leveled beyond Level 240 using Gold, Season Manuals, Season Essence, and Season Starlight.

### Affinity

Heroes gain affinity through Battles, Affinity Items, or by interacting with them in the Resonating Hall. Affinity provides no gameplay changes to heroes. Affinity provides rewards for reaching certain milestones:

| Affinity Level | Reward |
|---|---|
| 100 | 50x Diamond |
| 700 | 50x Diamond |
| 1300 | 1x Invite Letter |
| 3000 | 100x Diamond |
| 4500 | 1x Invite Letter |
| 8000 | 1x Invite Letter |

In total, achieving 8000 Affinity on any hero grants 200x Diamond and 3x Invite Letter.

### Ascension

Heroes have ascension tiers. A-Level heroes start at Elite tier and S-Level heroes start at Epic tier and can be ascended to higher tiers using their respective Soul Sigils or Acorns at specific tiers. Chippy and Hammie are Rare tier heroes that cannot be ascended.

### Hero Focus

Heroes unlock their Hero Focus when their ascension tier reaches Legendary+. Hero Focus skills provide a buff to certain stats in battle. The skill can be upgraded using Tidal Essence.

| Level | Required to Level |
|---|---|
| 1 | 10x Tidal Essence |
| 2 | 20x Tidal Essence |

### Exclusive Equipment

Heroes have an Exclusive Equipment that unlocks when their ascension tier reaches Mythic+. Exclusive Equipment can be upgraded using Tidal Essence, Temporal Essence, and Twilight Essence.

| Levels | Essence |
|---|---|
| +1 to +5 | 150x Tidal Essence |
| +6 to +10 | 75x Temporal Essence |
| +11 to +15 | 200x Temporal Essence |
| +16 to +20 | 125x Twilight Essence |
| +21 to +25 | 500x Twilight Essence |
