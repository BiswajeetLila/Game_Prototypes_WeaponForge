## Art Direction Notes

**Overall Style**: Painterly cel-shaded 3D with storybook/Ghibli influences. Soft-focus backgrounds, richly colored characters, exaggerated proportions with large expressive eyes.

**Vaduso Mountains biome**: Sandy beige paths, crumbling yellow-gold stone columns/ruins, blue-purple rock formations, warm atmospheric haze, ancient oval track structure.

**Oblivion Valley biome**: Lush green tree canopy, brown dirt path, dappled sunlight, soft bokeh foliage.

**Dream Realm arena**: Teal seafoam floor tiles, yellow sandstone cubic rocks (left wall), teal stone arch columns (right), amber torch accent. Clean theatrical boss-room staging.

**Holy Temple Cave (Elite)**: Near-black stone colosseum, rubble floor, purple/violet glowing pillar torches framing arena entrance, arched windows in background stone. Dramatic single-point top lighting on boss.

**World shrine environment**: Greek/Roman white stone, circular multi-step altar with gold inlay, colonnade, turquoise water adjacent, moss and wildflowers.

**Character art**: Storybook illustration style with fabric texture, flowing hair, ornate accessories. All heroes cast visible drop shadows. Boss designs: stone golem (gem joints + claws), organic tree creature (leaf crown + bark), wolf/beast mobs.

**Color branding**: Warm amber/gold + teal/cyan as primary contrast pair throughout all modes. Faction accent: purple (Graveborn), red-orange (Mauler), green (Wilder), blue (Lightbringer).

**UI palette**: Cream/parchment aesthetic, rounded corners, serif decorative titles, green buttons for primary actions, orange/gold for secondary. Victory screens use warm orange gradient. Resonance Hall uses deep gold/brown with ornate architectural backdrop.
