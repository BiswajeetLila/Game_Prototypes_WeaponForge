# Playtest Feedback June 24th

## UX:

1. Allow players to move tiles once placed on the board

1. Improve readability of Clusters (show a border around clustered tiles) and Modifiers (show which tiles have been modified by what, and what does it do now)

2. Improve readability of damage per rune, Merged rune, and Modified Rune Clusters. Players should be able to ascertain how much dps increases per gold in shop, before they decide on a strategy

3. Improve readability of shop and gold used/remaining.

4. Improve readability of telegraphed enemy weaknesses, to help strategize better against them.



## Game Mechanics

1. Allow cross hero merging of elements (only intra hero possible right now)

2. Remove or Modify the "Star" and "Splitter" keys. Make something with cooler outputs

3. Heroes prioritize firing in their own lane, and once they defeat their enemies, can start attacking the next lane to help out their squad

4. Have meaningful decision making moments againt enemies with resists/weaknesses.

5. Have more elements and Recipes, right now only fire, water and steam dont provide enough cool combos
